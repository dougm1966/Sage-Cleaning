## Services
1. **Residential Cleaning** — Regular home cleaning (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly). Kitchens, bathrooms, living areas, bedrooms.
2. **Commercial Cleaning** — Office buildings, retail, small businesses. Scheduled and one-time.
3. **Deep Clean** — Top-to-bottom intensive clean. Ideal for first-time clients, seasonal refresh, or pre-listing.
4. **Move-In / Move-Out** — Comprehensive cleaning for transitions. Landlord-ready results.
5. **Vacation Rental / Airbnb** — Turnover cleaning between guests. Quick turnaround, hotel-quality results.

## Markets Served
- **Homeowners** — Busy families who want their time back
- **Renters** — Move-in/out deposit protection
- **Small Businesses** — Professional, clean work environment
- **Vacation Rental Owners** — Airbnb/VRBO hosts needing reliable turnover
- **Real Estate Professionals** — Pre-listing and post-closing cleans
- **Property Managers** — Ongoing maintenance of multiple units

## Service Area
Big Horn Basin, Wyoming — including Worland, Basin, Greybull, Lovell, and surrounding communities.

## About / Story
SAGE Cleaning was founded to bring reliable, high-integrity cleaning services to the Big Horn Basin. The name reflects the landscape — sage, clean, natural. Every cleaner is vetted and trustworthy. The team is local, invested in the community, and committed to excellence.
