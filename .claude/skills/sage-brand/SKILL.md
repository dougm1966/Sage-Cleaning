## Brand Colors

## Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (serif) — Google Fonts
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif) — Google Fonts
- **Font weights:** 400 (body), 600 (subheadings), 700 (headings, CTAs), 400i (italic accents)

## Brand Voice
- Warm, trustworthy, professional — not corporate
- Local pride: "Big Horn Basin" is a point of identity
- Family-oriented messaging: "Your time is for family, not chores"
- Never use cleaning jargon. Plain language.

## Taglines
- "The Big Horn Basin's Best Cleaning Experience"
- "We handle the mess — you handle what matters"

## Tone Rules
- CTA buttons: action verbs — "Get A Quote", "See Our Services", "Call Us Today"
- Never: "Submit", "Click Here" as a primary CTA
- Headlines: sentence case for body, title case for hero H1 only
